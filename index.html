What We Do

The Complete Roofing Service

New builds, re-roofs, heritage restoration. If it's on a roof, we do it.

Slating

Welsh, Spanish, Canadian, South American. Salvaged Blue Bangor for listed buildings. We stock, source, and advise.

Tiling

Canopies, porches, new builds, matching existing. If it can't be sourced in Ireland, we'll import it.

Flat Roofing

Belgian membranes, 25-year guarantee, rock crystal finishes, full insulation. No silver paint needed.

Restoration & Heritage

Georgian, Edwardian, listed buildings. We're fully certified and have worked on some of Dublin's finest.

Copper, Zinc & Lead

25+ years of coppersmith experience. Valleys, dormers, step flashings, copper ridge tiles.

Gutters, Fascia & Soffits

Cast iron, plastic, aluminium. Fascia from scrolled timber to PVC. We supply, fit, and replace.

Old Skills. Modern Times.

P. Rooney Roofing was established in 1935 and is now a third generation family business. We've built our reputation the only way that matters: through the quality of our work.
Fully certified in Georgian and Edwardian period roofing and heritage conservation. From a small residential repair to a full restoration, every job gets the same standard of care.
